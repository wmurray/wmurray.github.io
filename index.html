Education

  • Printmaking - Linoleum and Copper Etching
  • Screen Printing
  • Color Theory
  • Design and Composition
  • Advanced Graphic Design Theory
  • Photography - Film and Digital
  • Digital Video editing
  • Web Design (HTML/CSS) and Flash

After receiving my degree, I applied my skills to some freelance work in photography and design and began to self-teach HTML, CSS, JavaScript/jQuery, and a little bit of Ruby. I was always reading some tutorial site, book, or lesson from CodeSchool.

Recently I decided to further my education with a solid foundation, so I applied to a Front End Web Development program through General Assembly. I am currently enrolled in the 12-week program which covers HTML and CSS, as well as diving into JavaScript, jQuery, and more. I look forward to completing this program in early February 2014 and perhaps continuing to study through General Assembly in the Back-End Web Development program.
