A little about me

Hi, I'm Will Murray. I am a web developer living and working in the Boston, Massachusetts area. In past lives, I was a design intern, a computer technician in a high-volume retail store, a photographer, a production worker in a factory, and a dishwasher for a cooking show. Now, I make websites.

When I am not coding, there is a handful of things you can find me doing. I enjoy board and video games, so you may find me on XBox Live, Battle.net, PSN, or at a game night in a local hobby shop. I also love to ride my motorcycle when the weather is in my favor, and will often do so with my camera bag strapped to the seat. Occasionally, you'll find me stooped over a steaming kettle brewing what may eventually become a drinkable beer.
